IMAGES FOLDER GUIDE
===================

Place your fitness-related images here.

RECOMMENDED FREE IMAGE SOURCES (No login required):

1. UNSPLASH.COM
   - Visit: https://unsplash.com/
   - Search: "fitness", "gym workout", "yoga", "health"
   - Download images for personal/commercial use
   - Suggested images:
     * hero-image.jpg (1200x600px) - Person exercising
     * gym-interior.jpg - Gym setting with equipment
     * trainer-photo.jpg (300x300px) - Personal trainer
     * yoga-class.jpg - Yoga session
     * healthy-food.jpg - Nutrition/diet

2. PEXELS.COM
   - Visit: https://www.pexels.com/
   - Search: "fitness workout health"
   - Free, no attribution needed
   - Good quality images

3. PIXABAY.COM
   - Visit: https://pixabay.com/
   - Search: "gym exercise training"
   - Free for commercial use
   - Excellent image quality

RECOMMENDED IMAGE SIZES:

- Hero Image: 1200x600px or wider
- Team Member Photos: 300x300px
- Facility Photos: 400x300px
- Testimonial Images: 100x100px

NAMING CONVENTION:

Use clear descriptive names:
- hero-image.jpg
- team-john-smith.jpg
- facility-gym.jpg
- facility-yoga.jpg
- facility-pool.jpg
- trainer-lisa.jpg
- trainer-maria.jpg

HOW TO USE IN HTML:

<img src="images/hero-image.jpg" alt="Description">
