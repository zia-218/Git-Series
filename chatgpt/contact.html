Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by calling us, emailing appointments@healthhub.com, or submitting the contact form with your preferred date and time.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 24 hours in advance receive a full refund. Late cancellations may incur a small fee.

Do you offer online consultations?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations for fitness coaching, nutrition counseling, and mental health support via video call.

What insurance do you accept?

We accept most major insurance plans. Please contact us directly to confirm if your insurance is accepted.

Are there membership packages available?

Yes, we offer various membership packages tailored to different needs and budgets. Contact us for detailed pricing.

How quickly will I see results?

Results vary based on individual circumstances. Most clients see noticeable improvements within 4-6 weeks with consistent effort.
